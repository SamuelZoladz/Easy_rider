Detailed Description

Tracks per-edge load and provides effective speeds/time for routing.

Effective speed model: v_eff = min(road.maxSpeed, speedLimit(edge)) * f(load) where f(load) is a piecewise factor in (0, 1].

Additionally, a very low speedLimit can emulate incidents or blocks.
