◆ run()

void SfmlSimulationVisualizer::run ( double  targetFps = 60.0)
overridevirtual

Drive the Simulation and rendering at a target FPS.

Uses a variable-step integrator: dtSim = realDeltaSeconds * timeScale(). The loop sleeps to cap the frame rate to targetFps (default 60).

Parameters
targetFpsDesired frames per second (min clamped to 1.0).
